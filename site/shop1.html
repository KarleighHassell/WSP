Headlight Tattoo

Headlight Tattoo is a New Jersey Corporation which was established in 1995 and is owned by Harry and Linda Ake. The original shop Headlight #1 located at 1086 Delsea Drive, Westville, NJ is still our busiest location and is a nastalgic and significant part of South Jersey History. Currently, the shop is operated by Harry Ake, Elizabeth O'reilly, Eric Dougherty, David Capece, and Kayla Capanna.We take walk ins and will be happy to schedule an appointment with a consultation and a $50 non refundable deposit.
